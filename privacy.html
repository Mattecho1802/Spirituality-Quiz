Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This privacy policy explains how we collect and use information when you complete the Spirituality Profile Quiz.

What we collect

When you complete the quiz we store the following anonymously:

  • Your quiz answers and scores
  • Your selected age group (if provided)
  • Your selected gender (if provided)

We do not collect your name, email address, IP address, or any information that could identify you personally.

Why we collect it

The data is used only to calculate and display group averages on the results chart, so you can compare your spirituality profile to others who have taken the quiz.

Who sees your data

Your individual answers are not displayed to anyone. Only anonymous group averages are shown. The data is stored securely using Supabase and is not sold or shared with third parties.

Cookies

This site does not use cookies to track you. If ads are displayed, the ad provider (such as Google) may set their own cookies — please refer to Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy for details.

Children

This quiz may be taken by people of all ages including children. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from anyone. Age group is collected only as an anonymous category for statistical purposes.

Your rights

Because we collect no personal data tied to your identity, we are unable to look up or delete a specific individual's submission. If you have concerns, please contact us at the address below.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact: spiritualityquiz@gmail.com
